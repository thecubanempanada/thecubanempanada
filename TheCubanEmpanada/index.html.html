About Catering


When I was a little girl, Sunday’s were family day. My dad would wake up early and go food shopping for our Sunday feast. I would always be under toe. I took my foot stool into the kitchen and he would teach me how to make the most amazing Cuban meals from the best black beans and savory picadillo, to the most delicious, succulent Cuban roast pork using only the freshest ingredients and homemade sofrito and mojo of course. I put all of the fresh ingredients and the passion that my father had for the food we served to our families into every empanada we will serve to you and your family.
